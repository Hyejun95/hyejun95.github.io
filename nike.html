Nike Connect

During my internship in Rhode Island, I've worked on creating an UIUX Design for Nike NFC mobile appication. Few years ago, Nike launched the Nike NBA Connected Jerseys, which allow customers to leverage NFC technology to tap their smartphone to the Nike products to unlock exclusive content. NikeConnect provides personal, next-level access to athletes, exclusive offers and the game on users' smartphone. NFC is the most seamless way to provide personalized mobile experiences.

Tools

Invision, Sketch
Indesign, Mokupframes

Search and Scan
Since the scanning the NFC chip is the most important feature of this app, I created a searching page for the chip and scanning it as first page part of design.
Personalized
After NFC chip is detected on Nike Product, NFC Connect reveals the product information along with a welcome message.
Exclusive Features
By unlocking exclusive content, users can now access to personal, next-level access to athletes, exclusive offers and the game.
