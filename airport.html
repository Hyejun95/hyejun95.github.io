A Beautiful Journey

As a metaphor for the diversity of experiences people encounter while traveling around the world, a large scale LED Media art installation features a series of dynamic travelling videos through the lens of a cat or dog at Incheon International airport in Seoul.

Using techniques such as automation and zeotrope, cats are depicted traveling to diverse destinations that gesture the buildings and landmarks of different countries. The adventures of these adorable animals result in an entertaining and friendly video that evokes curiosity to travellers, and engenders hope and excitement for their trips.
