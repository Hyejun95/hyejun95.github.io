Bubble

The goal of this project is to create an playful and satisfying visual effect that could be displayed on a cell phone screen. I was fascinated by soothing and relaxing effect of an ASMR that has been viral for few years. I created a jelly-like bubbles that expand and squish, creating a three dimentional space for ASMR effect on a lock screen

Tools

Cinema 4D, After Effect
